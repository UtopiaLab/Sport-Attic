Introduction

Tennis is a popular sport played individually or in doubles with a racket and ball. The objective is to hit the ball over the net and within the opponent's side of the court. It is a physically demanding sport that requires speed, agility, and endurance. Professional tennis players compete in tournaments throughout the year, with the Grand Slam events considered the most prestigious. Tennis is also enjoyed as a recreational activity, and tennis clubs and facilities are found in many communities around the world. Overall, tennis is a dynamic and exciting sport with a rich history and a bright future.
